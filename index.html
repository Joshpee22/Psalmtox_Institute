We are the best Music college in Lagos Nigeria

Psalmtox Music Institute is one of the best music schools and rapidly growing musical training centers in Lagos. We run courses for individuals from 7yrs - 35yrs; across the upper primary, Secondary, Sixth grade, Diploma, and Professional certifications. Our Services Include: Musical Instruments training: Keyboard, drums, violin, sax, trumpets, etc. Voices - Vocal training, ear training, composition, songwriting, etc. Performances: Prep for Auditions, Concerts, Dance and choreography, etc. Music Production: Digital Audio Productions, Sound Tech etc. Music Business Professional Courses - Concert Production, Artist Management, Tours, Royalties, and Contracts Musical Instrument Sales and Installations We offer classes from Basic Music Training to an Advance Diploma Music Certification, with examination certificates with the ABRSM, ROCK SCHOOL, etc. Our Classes are physical & Virtual, Home classes with 100% Theory and Practical; Classes hold on weekdays & weekends." Classrooms and Studios

Classrooms are where formal instruction takes place, led by experienced faculty members. Studios may refer to specialized rooms for individual or small group lessons, equipped with audio-visual technology or specific instruments tailored to the curriculum.

Music Library

A resource center housing a vast collection of sheet music, books, recordings, and digital materials relevant to music education and research. It provides students with access to a wide range of musical repertoire and academic resources to support their studies.

Practice Equipment

Additional tools and equipment provided to aid students in their practice routines. This may include metronomes, music stands, tuners, recording devices, or software applications designed to enhance learning and performance.
