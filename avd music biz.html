APPLY NOW

Music Business NGN 600,000.00
Course Duration: A Year

Course Summary
This program is tailored for *trained musicians* who seek to scale up their lifelong music career by establishing a Music Record Label, Manage Artists, Setting up concerts, or Show Biz This program exposes students to the basics of: • Record Labels, • Recording Deals, • Recording Royalties, • Contracts, Copyright and Music Laws, • Band tour and management, • Artist management, AR, Management and Leadership Training etc. In this program, Students will be exposed to all aspects of Music Management, Performance & Production. Benefits: Students who run this course will stand a chance of employment and affiliation with PMI in setting up their own Music Business. Duration: 1 Year Lecture Holds:* Three times weekly Sessions: Morning, afternoon, and weekend sessions are available. Course Fee:* Six Hundred and Thousand Naira (N600,000.00). Side Attractions: Assessments, Projects, Internships, and certifications are available with Job opportunities. NB: Payment must be made in full before the commencement of lectures.
